A Site for Knitting

A guide to knitting is a website designed to help guide you on your knitting journey. Here you'll find resources for knitting terminology, brief overview of knitting from around the world, and a bibliography to point to even more resources.

About the Author

Amanda has been a knitter for over twenty years. She learned knitting from her mother and grandmother who in turn learned knitting from their mothers and grandmothers. Having made dozens of garnments, home goods, and other fun knit objects she decided to share her knowledge! Through her years of knitting she's learned not only practical skills but about the history and sociology of knitting, taking her passion of knitting to a new level.
