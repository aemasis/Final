Knitting Resources Bibliography

History Books

  • Rutt, Richard. A History of Hand Knitting. Interweave Press, 2003.
  • Nargi, Lela. Knitting Around the World: A Multistranded History of a Time-Honored Tradition. Tuttle Publishing, 2009.
  • Fryer, Linda G. Knitting by the Fireside and on the Hillside: A History of the Shetland Hand Knitting Industry c. 1600-1950. Shetland Times Ltd., 1982.
  • Zimmermann, Elizabeth. Knitting Around, or, Knitting Without a License.
  • Kiewe, Heinz Edgar. The Sacred History of Knitting.
  • Hinchcliffe, Frances. Knit One, Purl One: Historic and Contemporary Knitting from the V&A's Collection.
  • Macdonald, Anne. No Idle Hands: The Social History of American Knitting.
  • Claßen-Büttner, Ulrike. Nalbinding: What in the World Is That? History and Technique of an Almost Forgotten Handicraft.
  • Levine, Barbara. People Knitting: A Century of Photographs.
  • Turnau, Irena. History of Knitting Before Mass Production.

  • Miller, Sharon. Heirloom Knitting.
  • Mucklestone, Mary Jane. 150 Scandinavian Motifs: The Knitter's Directory.
  • Gilpin, Di, and Sheila Greenwell. Gansey Knitting Sourcebook: 150 Stitch Patterns and 10 Projects for Gansey Knits.
  • Buller, Kate. Ultimate Knitter's Guide.
  • Nargi, Lela. Knitting Around the World. Threads, 95 pages. Newtown, CT: Taunton Press.
